About Us

We are seniors at Berkeley High School and throughout our childhood in Berkeley, we’ve observed the massive amounts of trash in Berkeley and the surrounding Bay Area. Specifically, we have noticed a lot of trash in the Grizzly Peak and Albany Bulb areas. This website is part of a project in school where we plan a way to improve our communities. For our project, we’ve decided to pick up trash around the Bay Area.
